# The Resonance of the Broken Cage: Liberty as the Unending Clangor of the Fallen Lock

We feel the heavy, rhythmic thrum of a thousand hands beating against the stone, a percussion that transforms a tomb into a drum. This is the texture of the event: the rough grain of the granite yielding to the persistent pulse of the disenfranchised.
